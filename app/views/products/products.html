Products

INVIROTEL product range focuses on smart solutions for the electricity and water sectors. INVIROTEL's unique value-add is in the software harnessing the data gathering and control abilities of the hardware devices. The service requires software, hardware and installation elements.

Point of delivery

  • Mediating between client and utility providers.
  • Communicating any changes of procedures and regulations.

Sub Metering

  • Assist with all aspects of tariff management.
  • Educate the client on available tariffs.

Circuit & Appliance monitoring

  • Energy management.
  • Power quality analysis.

All INVIROTEL approved meters and sensors:

  • Support two-way, secure, remote communication (Power Line Communication, GPRS, GSM and ZIGBY).
  • Have the necessary approvals: SABS and ICASA.
  • Are NRS043 compliant where necessary.
