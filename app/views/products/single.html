Single Phase Meters

M180R - Load Control 80A Meter

  • 20(80) A, Class 1, direct measurement static watt-hour meter.
  • Real two-way “A” band Power Line Communication.
  • Calculates locally TOU consumption at multiple tariffs.
  • Internal 100A load switching element.
  • Remote disconnection.
  • Remotely limits customer maximum power demand.
    • For meter operated with separate CIU (split display) unit:
  • Prepayment STS (Standard Transfer specification) operation, simple & TOU (Time Of Use).
  • Keypad entry for prepaid energy, STS tokens.
  • Reconnection controlled by customer.
