AMI

Advanced metering infrastructure (AMI) is an integrated system of smart meters, communication networks and data management systems that enables two-way communication between utilities and customers.

The concentrator is a Central Processing Unit (CPU) for remote reading of electricity and water meters. The CPU communicates via Power Line Communication (PLC) to the electricity meters and via Radio Frequency (RF) to the water meters. The concentrator allows two-way communication access to all meters and can have up to 400 meters connected to it.

Meter Management

  • No more manual reading of meters.
  • Accurate, real time readings.
  • Mobile access of information and control of electricity, water and gas.

Portfolio Management

  • Centralized management system for an entire portfolio of properties.
  • The portfolio manager can monitor and manage properties individually, regionally or across the entire portfolio.
  • Controlled and gradual cut-off for non-payments.

Savings & Recovery

  • Controlled and gradual cut-off for non-payments.
  • Allows for faster outage detection and restoration of services.

Alerts

  • Tamper, bypass, leakage and power failure-reported via Email and SMS.

Tenant Empowerment

  • Tenants can access their usage data any time through the internet.
  • Tenants now have control of their own consumption.

Carbon Emissions

  • Accurate carbon emission reports.
  • Implementation of carbon reduction programs.
