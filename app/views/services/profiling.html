Profiling, Reporting & Analysis

Pre-paid vending of electricity to INVIROTEL pre-paid meters is facilitated either by automatic remote charging of the meters by the vended amount or by the generation of STS compliant tokens.

Profiling-Circuit and Appliance Monitoring

  • Measurement every 5 seconds or longer.
  • Power, consumption, power factor, reactive energy, carbon emissions, frequency and voltage all measured.
  • Individual circuits, lights and appliances all measured.
  • Accurately identifying energy wasting circuits and appliances.

Reporting

  • Comprehensive secure automated reporting.
  • Reports can be integrated.
  • Scenario planning and budgeting.

Analysis

  • Powerful real time statistics through graphs and charts.
  • Comprehensive and unique insight into consumption and billing.
  • Load control and demand management.
  • Accurate Carbon emission details.
