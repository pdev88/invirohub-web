About Us

EMS INVIROTEL Energy Management (Pty) Ltd is an integrated smart electricity, water metering and propriety software company. Invirotel provides smart metering, monitoring and evaluation tools for the Public, Private as well as the Mass Residential sectors.

Products

INVIROTEL product range focuses on smart solutions for the electricity and water sectors. INVIROTEL's unique value-add is in the software harnessing the data gathering and control abilities of the hardware devices. The service requires software, hardware and installation elements.
